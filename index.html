🦠 Biosecurity Warning Signs

Ask Jalil about these every morning:

  • Sneezing, coughing, or rattling sounds
  • Watery, green, or blood-stained droppings
  • Lethargic, hunched birds not eating
  • Swollen eyes, face, or unusual symptoms
  • Sudden drop in egg production or feed intake
⚡ If any sign appears: Isolate bird → Photo it → WhatsApp Alex → Alert owners immediately
